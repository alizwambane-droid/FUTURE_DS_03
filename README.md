## 📈 Key Findings

- 🏆 **Best Converting Channel:** Referral
- 📉 **Lowest Converting Channel:** Social Media
- 💰 **Highest Revenue Channel:** Paid Ads
- ⚠️ **Biggest Drop-off Stage:** Impressions → Clicks
- 📊 **Overall Conversion Rate:** <1% (impressions to purchases)

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Increase budget allocation to the highest-converting channel (Referral)
- A/B test landing pages to improve the Clicks → Signups conversion
- Add incentives (free trials, discounts) at the Trials → Purchases stage
- Retarget users who dropped off at the Signups stage with email campaigns
- Optimize ad creatives for the lowest-performing channel or pause it
